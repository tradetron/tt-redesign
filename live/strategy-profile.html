About this strategy

A defined-risk weekly iron condor on NIFTY — sells an OTM call+put spread to harvest theta, with protective wings. Market-neutral, exits intraday.

The strategy profits when NIFTY stays inside the short strikes through the weekly expiry, collecting time decay on the sold options. The bought wings cap the maximum loss on either side, so capital at risk is known up front and does not run away on a gap move. Positions are squared off the same session — no overnight exposure.
