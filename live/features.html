One-glance health across every live strategy. Green / amber / red, with a plain-English line beneath: "Today: 2 strategies fired 4 trades, no issues, broker reconciled at 15:30." If something's off, you know in a second.

Drawdown breach, broker disconnect, order rejection, anomaly detected — straight to your phone. You decide what counts as "wake me up." Default-on for the things that hurt; opt-in for the rest.
