FAQ

Common questions.

What is a Stockbag?

A carefully curated portfolio of stocks selected based on a specific theme. Equal-weight, market-cap, momentum, value — any rule you can express.

What are the benefits of investing?

Diversification across multiple stocks, theme-driven exposure, and automatic rebalancing in your own broker.

Can I customise a Stockbag I subscribe to?

No — the creator's logic is fixed by them. But you can create your own Stockbag for your personal use, any time.

How are Stockbags managed?

Stockbags you create are managed by you. Stockbags you subscribe to are managed by their creator — reach out to them for the rebalance schedule.

Minimum investment?

Varies by Stockbag — each has its own minimum based on its stock composition. Check the listing for details.

How often is it rebalanced?

Set by the creator. Some run monthly, some quarterly, some on threshold triggers. Deploy on paper trade to see the rhythm.

Any fees?

An active Tradetron subscription is required to execute a Stockbag live. Paper deployments are free. Creators may charge a fee — you'll see it on the listing.

Can I withdraw any time?

Yes. Go to the deployed Stockbag, select Withdraw, and input the amount. Settlement happens in your own broker.
