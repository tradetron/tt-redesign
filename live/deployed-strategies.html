Pause strategy

New signals will be ignored while paused. Open positions are unaffected and will continue to run as usual.

You can resume any time from the deployed-strategies list.

Exit strategy

All open positions will be squared off at market price. The strategy will stop generating new orders and move to Exited state.

Download data

Export this strategy's runtime data and order book.

Switch to Lite execution

Lite mode evaluates conditions less frequently and skips deep position book sync. Useful for low-frequency strategies — saves compute and reduces broker API hits.

Switching only affects the next iteration onward. Open positions and pending orders stay intact.
