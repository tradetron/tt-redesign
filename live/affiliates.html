Common questions
Who can become an affiliate?
Anyone with a Tradetron account. You don't have to be a paying subscriber yourself, though most of our best affiliates trade on the platform too — it shows in the content they make.
What counts as a referral?
A new user who signs up through your link within the attribution window and goes on to pay for a subscription. Paper-trading-only users don't generate commission, since they don't pay us either.
How and when do I get paid?
Commissions accrue daily and settle on the 7th of each month, to your nominated bank account or PayPal. No minimum threshold — if you earned ₹500, it pays out. We cover bank transfer fees.
Is the commission really recurring?
Yes — you earn your tier's percentage every month a referred user keeps paying, for their first six months (longer at Partner and Ambassador tiers). If they upgrade plans, your share scales with them.
Can I run paid ads on the Tradetron brand?
No bidding on Tradetron brand keywords, and no using the marks in a way that implies you are Tradetron. See the Trademark page for specifics. Honest content marketing — reviews, tutorials, comparisons — is encouraged.
What if my referral cancels?
Your commission for that month stays paid; future months stop. We don't claw back past payouts. If they re-subscribe within 90 days, they're re-attributed to you automatically.
