01 · Claude

Add Tradetron as a custom connector.

Pro · Max · Team · Enterprise No Tradetron tokens used

Claude's paid plans let you add a custom connector. Add Tradetron once and the strategy-building tools are available in every chat — you only pay your normal Claude subscription, no Tradetron tokens consumed.

MCP Server URL https://mcp.tradetron.tech/mcp
Authentication OAuth — sign in with your Tradetron account
[ 01 ]
Open Connectors
In Claude, open Settings → Connectors and click Add custom connector.
[ 02 ]
Name & paste URL
Set Name to Tradetron, paste the Remote MCP server URL above, then click Add.
[ 03 ]
Authorize with OAuth
Click Connect. A Tradetron sign-in window opens — log in and Authorize access.
[ 04 ]
Start chatting
Enable the Tradetron connector in a chat, then ask — e.g. "Create a Nifty short straddle with a 30% stop-loss."

Custom connectors require a paid Claude plan. Exact menu labels may vary slightly by plan and region.

06 · Strategy Audit

Audit the strategy on every AI channel.

Available on all 5 channels Pre-deploy & post-build

Building is half the job. After a strategy is created, ask any AI to audit it — plain-English diff between what you described and what actually got built. The same audit runs on Claude, ChatGPT, WhatsApp, Slack and Telegram, against the same engine that powers the Go-Live audit on your dashboard.

You audit strategy 12345
Claude You said: NIFTY short straddle, ATM, 30% SL on each leg, square off 15:15. Strategy does: short CE + short PE at ATM, exit 15:15. ✓ Gap: 30% stop-loss legs are missing on both options. ⚠ Recommend: add SL legs before Go-Live.
[ 01 ]
What the audit does
A plain-English, field-by-field diff between your original intent and what got built — entries, exits, hedges, SL legs, strikes, expiries, lot multipliers. Lifted from the same engine that gates Go-Live.
[ 02 ]
Why it matters
Catches silent drops — a missing SL leg, a wrong strike, a mismatched expiry — before they show up in your live PnL. The most expensive bug is the one you don't know about.
[ 03 ]
Same call, every channel
"audit 12345" on WhatsApp, /audit 12345 on Telegram, or just ask Claude / ChatGPT / Slack — the strategy id is enough. Output is identical across surfaces.
