Strategy Building Wizard

Build a wide gamut of strategies — without writing a line of code.

Tradetron's wizard lets you compose, backtest and deploy algorithmic strategies visually. What you build, you can also sell — to other traders, brokers and investors through the marketplace.

  1. [ 01 ]
    The trading engine

    Your conditions. Our execution.

    Set your entry, repair and exit conditions. The engine checks them tick by tick — the moment any condition is satisfied, the trades fire into your broker. Independent parts, working across servers, hosted on the cloud to remove single-point bottlenecks.

  2. [ 02 ]
    Multiple sets per strategy

    Mini hedge fund inside one strategy.

    Each set has its own entry / repair / overnight / exit logic — plus a universal repair and universal exit that override every set when triggered. Compose a portfolio of independent algorithms behind one deployment.

  3. [ 03 ]
    Underlying list

    One strategy. Many instruments.

    Per set, choose a single underlying or a list of underlyings on which your conditions run. Apply technical analysis, fundamental screens and options logic across an entire basket without writing a single line of code.

  4. [ 04 ]
    Position Builder

    Beyond fixed strike, expiry, quantity.

    Most ready-made option tools force you to lock strike, expiry and qty up front. Tradetron lets you express each as a custom formula using the keyword library — so your position adjusts as the market moves.
