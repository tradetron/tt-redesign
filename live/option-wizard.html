The problem it solves

Options trading without the screen-glued anxiety.

Crafting a strategy from scratch and executing four legs manually into a broker terminal — no SL, no target, no automation — is a rollercoaster. It's a cycle of profits, near-misses, missed exits, and screen-time you didn't sign up for. Options Wizard takes the structural decisions you've already made and turns them into a running algorithm in about two minutes.

What it is

Tradetron's Options Wizard.

A feature for option traders to quickly build algorithmic option strategies. Choose from a list of pre-defined structures — straddles, strangles, iron condors, butterflies, calendars, ratios — and customise strikes, target prices, stop-loss orders, trailing SL, and overall MTM cuts. Backtest, paper trade, or deploy live, in any of the 100+ supported brokers.

[ 01 ] Pre-defined option structures

Straddles, strangles, iron condors, butterflies, calendars, ratio spreads — start from a structure your brain already speaks.

[ 02 ] Modify the defaults

Customise strikes and per-leg details to match your trading preferences. Same wizard, your numbers.

[ 03 ] Per-leg + portfolio risk

Target prices, stop-loss, trailing stop-loss — applied at the leg level or the strategy level. Overall MTM profit and loss cuts to lock the day.

[ 04 ] Pre-defined exit conditions

Time-based, price-based, MTM-based exits, ready to drop in. Automate the decision before you're in the moment.

[ 05 ] Backtest enabled

You can now backtest Option Wizard strategies on years of real option-chain data — holidays, results, IV / HV all in.

[ 06 ] Execute in your broker

Wizard-built strategies deploy directly to your brokerage account. Tradetron never touches your money.

Advanced

Indicator-based conditions, when you need them.

Need RSI, ADX, supertrend, VWAP, ALMA on top of the option structure? Take the wizard-built strategy into the Advanced Strategy Builder and layer indicator-based entries on the wizard's legs. Note: once you do, the strategy won't reopen in the wizard (except for advanced or marketplace settings).

FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need algo-trading experience?

No. The Options Wizard is designed to be accessible to both beginners and experienced traders. Prior experience helps but isn't required.

Is there a cost?

Tradetron's pricing applies — see the pricing page for current tiers.

Can I modify the pre-defined strategies?

Yes — customise strikes, leg quantities, hedges and exits. Same structure, your numbers.

How do I execute the strategies?

Directly through your brokerage account. Tradetron orchestrates execution; orders go into your connected broker via official API.
