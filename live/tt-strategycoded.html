04 · FAQ

Common questions.

Can you code any type of strategy?
Almost certainly. We specialise in TradingView integrations, indicator-based strategies, Python projects, trailing-stop logic, and multi-strategy templates. Share your requirements — we'll assess feasibility upfront.
How much does it cost?
Depends on complexity. Most projects fall in ₹5,000 – ₹50,000. You get a fixed quote and a timeline after the consultation, before any payment.
How long does it take?
Most strategies are delivered in 1–2 weeks. Timeline is locked when scope is finalised.
Is my data and logic confidential?
Yes. Your strategy stays private — used only to build what you asked for. We don't reuse logic or list strategies without permission.
What support comes after delivery?
Test the strategy in paper trading. Any issues you find — your developer resolves them. We don't ship and disappear.
Important notice on payments
Tradetron employees never request payment to personal accounts. If someone asks you to, report it via chat support immediately.
