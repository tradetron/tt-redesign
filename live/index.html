The part that matters

Your money. Your broker. Your control.

The single most common question we get is "is my money safe?" Here's the honest answer: we never hold it.

[ 01 ] Funds never leave your account

Every order is placed into your own brokerage via its official API. Tradetron never custodies, withdraws, or even touches your capital.

[ 02 ] Kill it in one click

Pause, exit, or sever the broker link instantly — from any device. You are never locked in, and you're always the one holding the off switch.

[ 03 ] Regulated brokers only

You connect to SEBI-registered (India) and regulated US brokers. Trading carries risk; past performance never guarantees future results.
