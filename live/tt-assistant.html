02 · What it does

Build, audit, deploy, watch.

Four jobs you'd otherwise click through the wizard, the audit screen, the broker connect dialog and the alerts panel for — done from one chat window.

[ 01 ]
Build by describing
"Short NIFTY ATM straddle, 50-point hedge, exit at 30% profit or 3% MTM SL." The assistant translates that into a valid Tradetron template — leg structure, conditions, exits — ready to backtest in your account.
[ 02 ]
AI Strategy Audit (pre-deploy)
Before the "Go Live" button activates, the assistant runs a plain-English audit: "you asked for X, the template does Y, here are the field-by-field matches and gaps." Catches silent drops — even when our own team built the strategy for you.
[ 03 ]
Risk alerts on WhatsApp
Drawdown breach, broker disconnect, order rejection, anomaly detected — straight to your phone. You decide what counts as "wake me up." Default-on for the things that hurt; opt-in for the rest.
[ 04 ]
Trust Score on demand
"How are my strategies?" — get a one-line health check across every live deployment. Green / amber / red, with the reason: "Today: 3 strategies fired 8 trades, no issues, broker reconciled at 15:30."
03 · What it feels like

Plain English in. Live strategy out.

No more wrestling with keywords you don't remember the names of. Tell the assistant what you want; it picks the right blocks, names the legs, sets the conditions, and shows you exactly what it built before anything goes live.

If the description is ambiguous — "stop loss on the short call" could be on premium or on underlying — the assistant asks one clarifying question instead of guessing. That's the whole game: no silent drops.
