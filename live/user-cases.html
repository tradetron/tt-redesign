[ 01 ] The Day-Job Trader
RK
Rohit K.
Pune · options seller · 9-to-6 day job

Rohit runs a short-strangle on Bank Nifty four days a week. He's in stand-ups at 9:15, in meetings through the open, and the only window he gets is lunch. Before Tradetron he managed the strategy by hand and missed two trades a week to meeting overruns.

Headline feature · Trust Score + WhatsApp Risk Alerts
A glance at his phone is all he needs.

The Trust Score on his Tradetron dashboard is green / amber / red — one glance and he knows if the bot is healthy, if all legs filled, if his broker reconciled. A WhatsApp alert finds him only when something he asked to be woken up for happens: drawdown breach, broker disconnect, order rejection. The other 99% of the day, he's at his desk doing his job.

[ 04 ] The Cost-Conscious Swing Trader
PR
Priya R.
Chennai · swing trader · ex-CA

Priya is an accountant by training. She runs a medium-frequency NIFTY swing strategy that looks profitable on gross numbers. For two quarters she couldn't reconcile her broker statement with her own spreadsheet — there was a gap and she couldn't name it.

Headline feature · True-Net PnL
28% of gross was disappearing into costs.

Tradetron's True-Net PnL strips out brokerage, STT, exchange fees, GST and realistic slippage — and shows what the strategy actually netted her after the broker took its cut. The cost-burn chart told her the truth: 28% of her gross profits were vanishing into transaction costs. She cut the trade frequency by half, kept the edge intact, and her real returns went up.
